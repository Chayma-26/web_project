lentil salad & fish

roasted veggies is always a good idea with grilled fish and dlentius salad

Ingredients

  1. grilled fish
  2. 1/2 cup of lentil
  3. tomatoes
  4. onion
  5. parsely & fresh mint
  6. cucumber
  7. 1 tbsp olive oil
  8. 1 tsp salt
  9. veggies(cauliflower,zucchini,eggplant)
  10. 1/2 cup lemon juice
Cook the lentils.Meanwhile, as the lentils are cooking, combine all of the lemon dressing ingredients in a small bowl and whisk together until combined.
Add the cooked and chilled lentils, cucumber, red onion, mint and tomatoes to a large bowl.Drizzle evenly with the lemon dressing and toss until evenly combined.
finally add the roasted veggies, grilled fish and enjoy your dish.

pumpkin mashed potatoes

What about pumpkin mashed potatoes and boiled eggs

Ingredients

  1. 2 eggs
  2. 1/2 cup potatoe
  3. 1/2 cup pumpkin
  4. tomatoes
  5. onion
  6. spinach
  7. 1 tbsp olive oil
  8. 1 tsp salt
  9. 1 tsp black papper
  10. parsely
Mash the pumpkin and potatoes than add olive oil, salt and black papper
saute the spinach and onion with some olive oil finally add the 2 boiled eggs and some sliced tomatoes.
