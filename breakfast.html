Porridge

what is the best way to start a new day ?
Making a delicious porridge bowl
with so much love

Ingredients

  1. 3 tbsp oats
  2. 1 tbsp chia seeds
  3. 1/2 cup of milk
  4. 1/2 cup of water
  5. 1 tsp honey
  6. 1 tsp cinnamon
  7. choclate
  8. fruits(banana/kiwi/raspberry)
Start by measuring the oats and liquids.
The usual serving size is 1/2 cup oats to 1 cup of water, milk or combination of both.
I like combining 1/2 a cup of water and 1/2 a cup of milk for the best consistency. Then place in the microwave without a cover on it.
Microwave on high for 2 minutes and the consistency should be perfect.

French Toast

who is a french toast lover ?

Ingredients

  1. 2 egg
  2. 4 tbsp milk
  3. 1 tsp cinnamon
  4. 1 tsp honey
  5. peanut butter
  6. dark choclate
  7. fruits(appel)
French toast is very easy:
Soak thick slices of bread in an egg and milk batter,
then fry them to crisp up the outside and cook the egg custard center.
then top it with what ever you want(peanut butter, dark choclate, honey...)
also you can add some fruits like (appel, banana...)

Do you like it?
